My Favorite Bands

By: Grant Pullen

February 16, 2025

King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard is and amazing, genre bending band. They were my most listened to artist last year because they drop so much music and it's all fire.

Cage the Elephant only drops heat. I've had the privilege of seeing them twice live and they put on a phenomenal show.

Of course I have to put a band that got me into rock. Led Zeppelin are absolute legends who inspired generations, including my own.
